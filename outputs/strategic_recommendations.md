## SECTION 6 — RISKS & CAVEATS

### Model Limitations

| Limitation | Impact | Mitigation |
|------------|--------|------------|
| **Gaussian copula understates tail correlation** | Capital may be understated by £349M (33%) | Stress test shows worst-case; apply 1.5x buffer to cat lines |
| **Zero-inflation instability** | PropCat/PolVio allocations vary ±48% across confidence levels | Percentile smoothing applied; treat ranges not point estimates |
| **Cyber systemic not modeled** | Cloud outage scenario could be 10x current capital | DO NOT grow Cyber without Cat XL protection |
| **Parameter uncertainty** | GPD shape ±0.1 swings capital 30-50% | Recalibrate annually; sensitivity test before major decisions |
| **Single-year view** | Ignores reserve development | Acceptable for pricing engine; not for reserving |

### Key Sensitivities

**1. If tail correlation is higher than modeled:**
- Portfolio capital could be £1,050M (not £763M)
- All lines become less accretive
- PropCat/PolVio become even worse
- **Mitigation:** This strengthens the "exit PolVio, cut PropCat" recommendation

**2. If RI renewal pricing worsens by 20%:**
- Total RI cost: £24.7M → £29.6M
- PropCat becomes uneconomic even at £15M GWP
- **Mitigation:** Lock in multi-year RI where possible; reduce exposure proactively

**3. If PropCat/PolVio pricing increases 25%:**
- Break-even moves closer but still not achieved
- PropCat: needs 123% → 98% increase
- PolVio: needs 266% → 241% increase
- **Mitigation:** Even aggressive repricing doesn't fix fundamental capital intensity issue

**4. If Cyber systemic event occurs (£150M loss):**
- Current 30% QS: net loss £105M (catastrophic)
- With £30M Cat XL: net loss £75M (very bad but survivable)
- **Mitigation:** DO NOT grow Cyber without Cat XL

### What This Model Does NOT Tell You

**This model assesses ACCRETION on a net-of-reinsurance basis given current pricing and capital allocation.** It does NOT consider:

1. **Expense allocation** — Lines may have different expense ratios (Cyber likely 60%+, PropCat likely 30%)
2. **Strategic value** — PropCat provides Lloyd's franchise; PolVio provides geopolitical coverage relationships
3. **Brokerage relationships** — Exiting lines may damage broker relationships for other classes
4. **Personnel implications** — Cutting GWP 40% may require redundancies
5. **Market cycle** — Current soft market may be bottom of cycle (but we must price for today)
6. **Regulatory requirements** — Lloyd's may require minimum cat participation

**These factors are OUTSIDE the scope of this model but must be considered in final decisions.**
